General Questions

What is the E-Complaint Portal?

The E-Complaint Portal is a digital platform that allows citizens to report local civic issues such as road damage, water leaks, streetlight problems, garbage collection issues, and other community concerns. It ensures transparency, accountability, and faster resolution of complaints through direct assignment to relevant departments.

Was this helpful?

Who can use this portal?

Any citizen can use this portal to report civic issues. There are three types of users:

  • Citizens: Can submit and track complaints
  • Department Officers: Can view and update complaints assigned to their department
  • Administrators: Can manage all complaints, assign them to departments, and generate reports
Was this helpful?

Is the service free to use?

Yes, the E-Complaint Portal is completely free for all citizens. There are no charges for registering, submitting complaints, or tracking their status.

Submitting Complaints

How do I submit a complaint?

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to the Submit Complaint page
  2. Fill in your personal details (name, email, phone)
  3. Select the complaint category and priority
  4. Enter the location and description of the issue
  5. Attach a photo if available (optional but recommended)
  6. Click "Submit Complaint"
  7. Save your complaint ID for tracking

What types of issues can I report?

You can report various civic issues including:

  • Road Damage (potholes, broken roads)
  • Water Leak (pipeline leaks, water supply issues)
  • Streetlight Out (non-functional or damaged lights)
  • Garbage Collection (waste management issues)
  • Drainage (blocked drains, flooding)
  • Electricity (power outages, electrical faults)
  • Safety Concerns (security issues, hazards)
  • Other civic issues

Do I need to attach a photo?

While attaching a photo is optional, it is highly recommended. Photos help department officers better understand the issue and prioritize it accordingly. Clear images can significantly speed up the resolution process.

Can I submit multiple complaints?

Yes, you can submit multiple complaints for different issues. Each complaint will receive a unique ID for tracking. However, please avoid submitting duplicate complaints for the same issue.

Can I edit my complaint after submission?

Currently, you cannot edit a complaint once it's submitted. If you need to make corrections or add information, please contact our support team at support@ecomplaint.gov with your complaint ID.

Tracking Complaints

How do I track my complaint?

Visit the Track Status page and enter your complaint ID. You'll see the current status, timeline, assigned department, and any updates from the authorities.

What do the different statuses mean?

Complaint statuses include:

  • Submitted: Your complaint has been registered in the system
  • Assigned: Complaint has been assigned to the relevant department
  • In Progress: Department is actively working on resolving the issue
  • Resolved: Issue has been fixed and verified

What is the average resolution time?

Resolution time varies based on issue type and priority:

  • High Priority: 2-3 days
  • Medium Priority: 5-7 days
  • Low Priority: 7-14 days

Complex issues may take longer. You'll receive updates throughout the process.

Will I receive notifications about updates?

Yes, you'll receive notifications when your complaint status changes. Make sure to provide a valid email address and phone number during submission. You can also check the status anytime using your complaint ID.

Account & Privacy

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only use your contact details for complaint-related communication and never share your data with third parties.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can submit complaints without creating an account. However, registering allows you to view all your past complaints in one place and receive personalized updates.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Technical Support

The website is not working properly. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  2. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
  5. If the issue persists, contact support at support@ecomplaint.gov

Can I use this portal on my mobile phone?

Yes! The E-Complaint Portal is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Simply open your mobile browser and visit the portal.

What image formats are supported for uploads?

You can upload images in JPG, JPEG, PNG, and GIF formats. The maximum file size is 5MB. For best results, use clear, well-lit photos that show the issue clearly.
