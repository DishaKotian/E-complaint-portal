About Our Project

Building a transparent and accountable community together

What is E-Complaint Portal?

The E-Complaint Portal is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities. It provides a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly way for community members to report local issues and track their resolution in real-time.

Our platform addresses common civic problems such as road damage, water leaks, streetlight malfunctions, garbage collection issues, and safety concerns. By centralizing complaint management, we ensure faster resolution and improved accountability.
