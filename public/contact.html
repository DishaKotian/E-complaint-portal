Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a complaint?

Click on "Submit Complaint" in the navigation menu, fill out the form with accurate details, attach an image if possible, and submit. You'll receive a unique complaint ID to track your issue.

How can I track my complaint?

Use the "Track Status" page and enter your complaint ID. You'll see the current status and timeline of your complaint.

What is the average resolution time?

Resolution time varies by issue type and priority. High-priority issues are typically addressed within 2-3 days, while others may take 5-7 days.

Can I update my complaint after submission?

Currently, you cannot edit a submitted complaint. However, you can contact support with your complaint ID for any corrections or additional information.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use secure encryption and follow strict data protection protocols. Your information is only used for complaint resolution purposes.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report road damage, water leaks, streetlight issues, garbage collection problems, electricity issues, drainage problems, safety concerns, and other civic issues.

Do I need to register to submit a complaint?

While registration is recommended for tracking multiple complaints, you can submit complaints without registering. However, you'll need your complaint ID to track the status.

Will I be notified about updates?

Yes, if you provide a valid email or phone number, you'll receive notifications when your complaint status changes.
