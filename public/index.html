Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you get started

How do I file a complaint?

Register/login, click "Submit Complaint", fill the form with details and photos, then submit. You'll receive a tracking ID instantly.

How long does resolution take?

Average resolution time is 5-7 business days. Critical issues are prioritized and resolved within 24-48 hours.

Can I track my complaint?

Yes! Use your complaint ID on the "Track Status" page to see real-time updates and department actions.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use SSL encryption, secure servers, and comply with government data protection standards.
